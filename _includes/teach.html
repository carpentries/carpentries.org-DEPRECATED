Teaching the Carpentries

Software and Data Carpentry teach foundational computational and data science skills to researchers through short, intensive workshops. Workshop organizers must be willing to follow our Code of Conduct.

Instructors

Both Software and Data Carpentry workshops are taught worldwide by certified instructors, all of whom have completed this two-day training course. Instructors are volunteers.

We have a body of Trainers (currently about 40) who run these workshops so that we can grow our instructor pool, and take Carpentry workshops to new countries and communities. Trainers are also volunteers.
