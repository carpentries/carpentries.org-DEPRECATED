About

  • What are the Carpentries?

    Software and Data Carpentry are sibling organizations who share a mission to teach foundational computational and data science skills to researchers.

  • What is a Carpentries Workshop?

    A Software or Data Carpentry workshop generally comprises two full days. The instructor live codes and learners follow along. Workshops can be any size. Workshops are run by a team. Ideally this will consist of a host (who is the principal local contact for the workshop), certified instructors (who present the material and lead the practical exercises), a lead instructor (who ensures the teaching aspects of the workshop go smoothly), and helpers (who provide assistance during practical sessions).

  • How are Software and Data Carpentry Managed?

    Software and Data Carpentry are non-profit organizations, each governed by a Steering Committee. From January 2018, Software and Data Carpentry will be a merged organization.
