All Carpentries Workshops are run by a team. Ideally this will consist of a host (who is the principal local contact for the workshop), certified instructors (who present the material and lead the practical exercises), a lead instructor (who ensures the teaching aspects of the workshop go smoothly), and helpers (who provide assistance during practical sessions).
